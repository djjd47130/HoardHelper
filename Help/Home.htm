JD Hoard Helper

A scripted tool to help you herd your huge horrendous hectic heap of a hoard, thus healing your hard drive's health.

Summary

  • Create libraries that point to your hoard of data
    • Separate by Movies, TV Shows, Music, etc.
  • Write scripts to help you herd your hoard
    • Query files and folders and their metadata
    • Perform various file operations such as MoveFile, CopyFile, DeleteDirectory, CreateDirectory, etc.
  • Schedule your scripts to automatically execute via the Hoard Helper service
  • Build and maintain an index of media metadata via the Hoard Helper service
  • Automagically organize your media into the folder structure of your liking

 

Application

The core of the system is the JD Hoard Helper application. This is where you will create and manage your scripts, libraries, and other settings.

Libraries
